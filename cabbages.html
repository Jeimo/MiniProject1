Cabbages&Condoms

“Business for Social Progress”.
Started as a small vegetable stand inside the premises of an association for population and community development,
thus the name 'Cabbages and Condoms' was born.


With its unique and catchy label together with their liberating interiors and mouth-watering food availabilities, Cabbages & Condoms Bangkok never fails to lure passersby and food enthusiasts to their shop. Condom takeaway is also one of their distinct strategies that most people are getting curious into.

From the popular Pad Thai Goong Sod (Stir-fried Rice Noodles with Shrimp) to authentic Som Tam (Green Papaya Salad), this restaurant gained its reputation and took pride on their variety of Thai cuisine and specialty dishes that widely caters to their 400-seater restaurant. We were able to try some signature dishes and we're totally coming back.

To date, this progressive restaurant can also be enjoyed in England and Japan.

Advocating for a responsible family planning and poverty reduction through their well-known campaign in primary health, education, HIV/AIDS, rural development, environment, and water usage; this award-winning restaurant is vested as one of the finest Thai restaurants in Bangkok and has been an attraction to both foreigners and locals in Thailand.

Know more about this famous restaurant through their website: http://cabbagesandcondomsbkk.com/ or reach them through 02 229 4610 to reserve your table.
