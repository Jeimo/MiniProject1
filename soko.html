Soko Truffles

What started as a little gig on the side remained strong until now
as it grows to be an inclusive chocolate shop for those who chose to live their lives as vegans.


Looking for vegan desserts? Soko Truffles is your best choice!

Sokotruffles is a student-owned chocolate shop offering both vegan and non-vegan truffles that will definitely satisfy your sweet tooth. The business started in 2017 as a way to earn a little extra while the owner and her siblings are studying.

The idea of offering vegan truffles sprung as the family tries on vegan dishes. The owner's sister, a then vegetarian transitioning her lifestyle to veganism, inspired the idea of offering vegan options as she advocates against animal exploitation as part of her healing. It then inspired the owner to apply the advocacy on her business.

What are you waiting for? Try their chocolates that will surely satisfy your cravings and take part in a bigger movement and someone's healing.

For inquiries and orders, please contact their Instagram account - https://www.instagram.com/sokotruffles/
