Con Amore

What is sweeter than a spoonful of sugar? Love!
And what’s better than feeling it? Eating it!

Started as a hobby then became a passion which turns into something wonderful and practically helpful. Con Amore is an online shop that offers home baked cupcakes and banana loaves. From an Italian phrase ‘Con Amore’ which literally means ‘With Love’, its home bakers always make sure that they bake their goodies fresh, clean and with love.

They have moist cupcakes with a variety of flavors topped with creamy and sweet buttercream frosting that will surely give you a sugary sweet rush. Available in Chocolate, Mocha and Red Velvet, these portions of love can definitely complete your parties of any kind!

Part of their offering is an unusual marriage of fruity banana and creamy cheesecake. These layers of batters will give you an array of flavors that blends perfectly with your tongue. Baked in a ready to serve aluminium pan to ensure freshness and preserve its original form. Binded with love, this decadent bread will surely fill your stomach and satisfy your pastry cravings!

Personally, this loaf of happiness is perfect for a cup of coffee while enjoying the sweetness of life. Good for sharing but no one’s gonna blame you for digging it all!

Their cupcakes are available per box of six. 150php for chocolates, 185php for red velvet and 190php for mocha! Can’t decide which one to try? They also have assorted flavors per box for 175php so you can indulge two of each flavor in one box of love.
As for the banana cheesecake bread, it is available for 200php per pan of 7”x3.5”. Super worthy for its price!
