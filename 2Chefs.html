2Chefs Kitchen

Good food, creativity and hard work— how a simple eatery turned into one of the best high-quality food and catering service providers in Manila.

Started as a humble eatery mainly focused on the grounds of Dapitan Sampaloc, 2 Chefs Kitchen was first managed by Chef Mark’s mom in 2008.
She built a reputation of creating delectable food offerings that are mouth-watering and affordable. When Chef Mark and his partner had the chance to manage the place in 2016, they leveled-up everything by renovating the place and giving its menu a new look and taste while still carrying the character his Mom started.

From a naive eatery it was, they turned it into a sophisticated brand with an array of food offerings from savory to sweets, from mains to desserts. In 2017, they officially started providing catering services for small to large events such as weddings, birthdays and debuts. Government offices, schools and private sectors are also within the reach of their service.

They also do venue styling that really matches the ambience to its food line up that of course depend on the venue, motif and requests of the clients.

What sets them apart from other food service providers? It’s the 2 Chefs overflowing passion mixed with their great partnership and dedication. As the name of the brand implies, this collaboration of two chefs provided a perfect balance of aesthetics and flavor. As one focuses on the taste and the other one focuses on style.


Like all other businesses, 2 Chefs Kitchen was also crippled by the dreaded pandemic. They were forced to stop operating because of the lockdown but it didn’t take a long time for them to revive and survive. They reopened with an even more offerings with a variety of styled and customized cakes, reconstructed savory dishes and the likes, but this time via online food business.

As the online businesses increased greatly by number during this pandemic, 2 Chefs Kitchen is one of the active brands who successfully ventured online while slowly reopening their physical store. They make sure they offer classic but trendy food and delicacies by actively promoting their page thru social media.


They offer a beaucoup of menu in party trays of Filipino and Italian cuisines with different sizes. Varieties of pastas, noodles or different proteins like beef, pork, fish and chicken are fresh and available. They also offer pre-made cake in a tub or in a can, customized cakes, cheesecakes, breads and desserts. Baked 4 Cheese Lasagna, Beef Caldereta and the classic Chocolate Moist Cake are the crowd’s favorite and a must have on your order list!

Know more about their food and servings offerings by visiting their Facebook http://www.facebook.com/2ChefsKitchen or Instagram @2chefskitchen and don’t forget to share with us your experience!
