Breakfast Story

We just had the best breakfast story ever! And if you are a breakfast person,
this is definitely going for you; but if you are not, well you might want to think about it again.

Bangkok’s most famous all-day breakfast and brunch diner called 'The Breakfast Story' totally masters in serving real plate of happiness
through their breakfast and brunch offering.

We were fortunate to try two of their signature dishes in their flagship store located in Sukhumvit 33 and just as expected, it did not disappoint. Here are the following menus that you might be interested to:

Breakfast Story, the mostly recommended choice, a big serving of 3 fluffy pancakes, 2 fried/scrambled eggs, strips of crispy thin-sliced bacon, soft-boiled potatoes, with butter and syrup. It comes with a mix of kiwi, grapes, and other fruits in a cup. For only 300THB++ you’re already full with this one. Pair it with a freshly squeezed orange juice and you’re perfect! We recommend this one too!

Their signature Hot Caffe Latte is also definitely gonna wake you up and of course breakfast isn’t complete without a cup of hot coffee. They have many choices of beverage so you can just pick what suits your liking.


Overall experience, their wide range of food selection is just one of the many things to like about them, the restaurant vibe is really not for a morning idle or lazy breakfast, everything is moving. Everyone is enjoying their food, taking photos, sharing meals, talking, laughing, and enjoying a morning with a well-served breakfast. 10/10 is a must.

Aside from their store in Sukhumvit 33, they also have in Ploenchit area. Give them a try and let us know your experience! Check out their website to know more about their service: http://www.breakfaststorybkk.com/
